Founder

"At the end of 2016, I was assigned to teach a number of Pakistani students who just moved to HK at a secondary school. After chatting with them and other Pakistani students who have lived in HK since they were born, I was very curious about Pakistani culture and so tried to search for further information via internet. Eventually, I joined the local tour launched by Kung Yung Koon and applied as a volunteer to teach the Pakistani mothers how to speak Cantonese and a tutor for the Pakistani students. Through the programme, I was assigned to some Pakistani families while gaining a lot of sweet memories and valuable working experience. Till now, I still keep in touch with some of them.

Therefore, I have established KYK Foundation to run this website to continue the missions of Kung Yung Koon (KYK).
