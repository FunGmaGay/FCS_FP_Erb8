We Empower Communities and Celebrate Diversity in Hong Kong.




Kung Yung Koon (KYK) is an non-profit organization for ethnic minorities that nurtures an integrated society where multi-races can adapt and obtain equity.

We work on empowering the functions of different family roles among South Asians in cooperation with schools, enterprises, organizations, and others.
